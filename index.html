Our mission is to provide a comfortable environment for adoptable cats until they find their furrever home. We have partnered with Friends for Life animal rescue and all adoptions are processed through their center. If guests aren't looking to adopt, they can enjoy cat therapy, which also helps socialize the cats.

Our cafe can only fit a limited amount of humans at once. More people would create a stressful environment for the kitties and would make a bad cat to human ratio. We encourage making reservations to ensure accommodations can be met at your desired time.

While most all other cat cafe's charge by the hour upwards of $10-$20 / person, we charge a flat fee of $5 / person for all the kitty therapy you need, and with no requirement for food or beverage purchase -- though once you see our cafe items we bet you may be inticed! Please note that typically weekends in the cafe are busier than weekdays.

The Adoption Process

We process adoptions applications on behalf of Friends for Life right in the cafe! The adoption fee is $95. If you're interested in adopting, you complete a FFL application and schedule an interview with a FFL adoption counselor.

Additionally, our cats can be taken home for a one-night "sleepover" to see how they interact in your home and see if you would be a good fit for each other. Ask us for more details if you'd like to host a sleepover!
